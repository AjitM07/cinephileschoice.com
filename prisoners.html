Prisoners

Director: Denis Villeneuve

Release Year: 2013

Genre: Thriller

Story

When the police take time to find Keller Dover's daughter and her friend, he decides to go on a search himself. His desperation leads him closer to finding the truth and also jeopardises his own life.

Review

Dark, gripping and deeply unsettling, Prisoners is one of the most intense thrillers of the decade. Prisoners is a gritty, incredibly well-acted, suspenseful, thought-provoking crime drama thriller. This movie keeps plenty of suspects in play, along with multiple plotlines running and plates spinning. Kidnapping thrillers often lull us into a sense of safety in the opening sequences, showing the normal rhythms of life that will soon be shattered.
