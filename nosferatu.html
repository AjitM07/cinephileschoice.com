Nosferatu

Director: Robert Eggers

Release Year: 2024

Genre: Horror

Story

In the 1830s, estate agent Thomas Hutter travels to Transylvania for a fateful meeting with Count Orlok, a prospective client. In his absence, Hutter's new bride, Ellen, is left under the care of their friends, Friedrich and Anna Harding. Plagued by horrific visions and an increasing sense of dread, Ellen soon encounters an evil force that's far beyond her control.

Review

Marvelously orchestrated by director Robert Eggers, Nosferatu is a behemoth of a horror film that is equal parts repulsive and seductive.Overall, a very good horror movie that establishes Robert Eggers as an auteur in the genre as he remakes a horror classic with his vision.Nosferatu is a masterclass in accessibility without compromise.
