Black Panther

Director: Ryan Coogler

Release Year: 2018

Genre: Superhero

Story

After his father's death, T'Challa returns home to Wakanda to inherit his throne. However, a powerful enemy related to his family threatens to attack his nation.

Review

Black Panther elevates superhero cinema to thrilling new heights while telling one of the MCU's most absorbing stories.“Black Panther” creates wonder with great flair and feeling partly through something Hollywood rarely dreams of anymore. Black Panther is a modern expansion of that idea, a picture for everyone that stresses the necessary meaning of the word everyone.
