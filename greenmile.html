The Green Mile

Director: Frank Darabont

Release Year: 1999

Genre: Drama

Story

Paul Edgecomb, the head guard of a prison, meets an inmate, John Coffey, a black man who is accused of murdering two girls. His life changes drastically when he discovers that John has a special gift.

Review

This movie is a true masterpiece. All in all the The Green Mile is a very touching drama, with the joys and sorrows of the life pictured with great skill. This is a compassionate and well-intended movie with outstanding direction. The pacing of the plot is very good, how every character had a reason of being onscreen and the soundtrack is so beautiful. Everyone acted so well. It's a magical film that truly makes you feel the emotion which it attempts to portray, and it's very similar in themes, tone, and style.
