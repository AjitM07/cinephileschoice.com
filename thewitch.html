The Witch

Director: Robert Eggers

Release Year:2015

Genre: Horror

Story

In the New England of the 17th century, a banished Puritan family sets up a farm by the edge of a huge remote forest where no other family lives. Soon, sinister forces then start haunting them.

Review

As thought-provoking as it is visually compelling, The Witch delivers a deeply unsettling exercise in slow-building horror.No stupid jokes and exaggerated action scenes. Slow and calm dialogues. I liked the way the witch was enacted too."The Witch" (2015), a period horror film, is generally considered a worthwhile watch, praised for its atmospheric horror, strong performances, and unique approach to the genre, though some find it slow-paced or not scary enough.
