Bohemian Rhapsody

Director: Dexter Fletcher, Bryan Singer

Release Year: Biopic

Story

With his impeccable vocal abilities, Freddie Mercury and his rock band, Queen, achieve superstardom. However, amidst his skyrocketing success, he grapples with his ego, sexuality and a fatal illness.

Review

Rami Malek's impersonation adds a kind of magic to this Queen-produced rock slog with a troubling moralistic subtext. With great chemistry, the cast delivers a lot of entertaining moments, both musically and dramatically. Brilliant use of an incredible soundtrack which is overwhelming. It's a feel-good movie that attempts to show the best of Mercury and the band while also showing the conflicts and problems and self-doubt and tragedy.
