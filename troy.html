Troy

Director: Wolfgang Petersen

Release Year: 2004

Genre: Adventure

Story

Paris, the prince of Troy, convinces the beautiful Helen to leave her husband, King Menelaus, and come with him. But this enrages the king, and he declares war on Troy along with all his allies.

Review

It really is a historical war movie rather tran an action flick. The battle and fights scenes are superb, some of the best you will see. This is an enjoyable, rewatchable film packed with spectacular battles, heroism, romance, gorgeous costumes, a sweeping Hollywood score, and massive set pieces. Petersen's movie is competently put together, doesn't drag and manages its unwieldy story reasonably well.
