Fight Club

Director: David Fincher

Release Year: 1999

Genre: Thriller

Story

Unhappy with his capitalistic lifestyle, a white-collared insomniac forms an underground fight club with Tyler, a careless soap salesman. Soon, their venture spirals down into something sinister.

Review

The endless replay-ability and overall enjoyment I get from this film is like no other. Its ungainly final twist and unreal violence sequences dim a film with a brilliant premise and rage that still stings. Out of all the movies...there isnt one like Fight club which has affected my outlook on life. Fight Club is an amazing movie and undoubtedly one of the best movies you will have seen. Edward Norton and Brad Pitt played their roles perfectly.
