Don't Look Up

Director: Adam McKay

Release Year: 2021

Genre: Drama

Story

Two low-level astronomers must go on a giant media tour to warn mankind of an approaching comet that will destroy planet Earth.

Review

Don't Look Up shows us what happens if we wait too long to interrogate our culture and ourselves, lose sight of truth, and fail to take action. McKay has made “Don't Look Up,” a very angry, deeply anguished comedy freak out about how we are blowing it. Adam McKay's laboured satire challenges political indifference to looming comet catastrophe but misses out on the comedy.
