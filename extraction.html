Extraction

Director: Sam Hargrave

Release Year: 2020

Genre: Action

Story

While battling his own demons, Tyler, a ruthless mercenary, accepts the offer of rescuing an Indian crime lord's teenage son from Bangladesh. However, when he arrives, a gruesome blood bath ensues.

Review

“Extraction” is a sure-fire winner, showcasing a powerful story of redemption, and introducing intriguing characters, some of whom may surprise viewers. Extraction is a fun watch to turn your brain off for 2 hours and was actually better than I expected. The action is bloody, visceral and frantic. Chris Hemsworth plays a super-tough mercenary on an all-guns-blazing mission to rescue a crime lord's kidnapped son.
