The Revenant

Director: Alejandro González Iñárritu

Release Year: 2015

Genre: Adventure

Story

Hugh Glass, a legendary frontiersman, is severely injured in a bear attack and is abandoned by his hunting crew. He uses his skills to survive and take revenge on his companion, who betrayed him.

Review

The natural landscape and some of the scenes are overwhelming and spectacular! The action is brutally tense and the story, while deliberately paced and very simple, immerses the viewer in how gripping it is, being intense and emotional. As starkly beautiful as it is harshly uncompromising, The Revenant uses Leonardo DiCaprio's committed performance as fuel for an absorbing drama. A spectacularly beautiful and well made film. It's incredibly good at immersing you in the setting.
