The Northman

Director: Robert Eggers

Release Year: 2022

Genre: Adventure

Story

Adventure awaits Prince Amleth, whose father was killed and mother was abducted by his ruthless uncle. However, the journey takes him through twists which unravel a dark truth about his family.

Review

"The Northman" is an impressive and immersive experience that succeeds in its atmosphere and performances. One of the better movies of the year that most people didn't see. It's dark, gritty, and visually beautiful!Its story and themes may not be the most original, but because this film is so technically flawless, the experience that you get in return is well worthwhile. “The Northman” is the kind of movie where even the mud has rage; it is a visceral film filled with codas to the inescapable darker regions of nature.
