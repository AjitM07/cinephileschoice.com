Lincoln

Director: Steven Spielberg

Release Year: 2012

Genre: Biopic

Story

Abraham Lincoln uses his powers as the president of the United States of America as he strives to abolish slavery and reunite his country during the Civil War.

Review

It's gorgeous, with a phenomenal script that captures and sets free the characters at the same time. It is a story about Abraham Lincoln's 13th Amendment fight could be a snoozer in the hands of anyone else except director Steven Spielberg and actor Daniel Day-Lewis. This movie is an absolute masterpiece by Danial Day-Lewis. It's the best possible portrayal that I believe we can get of Abraham Lincoln. “Lincoln” is a rough and noble democratic masterpiece — an omen, perhaps, that movies for the people shall not perish from the earth.
