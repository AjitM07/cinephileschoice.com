Zack Snyder's Justice League

Director: Zack Snyder

Release Year: 2021

Genre: Superhero

Story

Bruce Wayne and Diana Prince try to bring the metahumans of Earth together after the death of Clark Kent. Meanwhile, Darkseid sends Steppenwolf to Earth with a vast army to subjugate humans.

Review

The film has an engaging story with a lot of heart and a great cast of characters who all have good chemistry together.Zack Snyder's Justice League is arguably a cohesive, consistent, and emotionally compelling movie. From start to finish the film is literary a new film with more story, more action, more thrill.The film is almost perfect on every level a modern masterpiece. A must watch for every one not just comic book and superhero fans. The visuals were mostly crisp and artful.
