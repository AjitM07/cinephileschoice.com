Ad Astra

Director: James Gray

Release Year: 2019

Genre: Adventure

Story

Astronaut Roy McBride undertakes a dangerous mission across the solar system to uncover the truth about his missing father and a series of strange power surges.

Review

Ad Astra is about humanity and our relationship with our fellow man. Pitt's performance is striking, the cinematography is breathtaking, the story is beautiful. This is rare, nuanced storytelling, anchored by one of Brad Pitt's career-best performances and remarkable technical elements on every level. Ad Astra is a stunning, thoughtful sci-fi drama starring Brad Pitt. The title is Latin for through hardships to the stars.
