John Wick

Director: Chad Stahelski

Release Year: 2014

Genre: Action

Story

John Wick, a retired hitman, is forced to return to his old ways after a group of Russian gangsters steal his car and kill a puppy gifted to him by his late wife.

Review

Stylish, thrilling, and giddily kinetic, John Wick serves as a satisfying return to action for Keanu Reeves. This is a bone-chilling thriller with non-stop action , including impressive fighting with a lot of blood and gore . It's a mindless but enjoyable for thrillers. ohn Wick is a slick, propulsive and ridiculous crime picture that strides like an automatic machine gun and has just as much subtlety. The film breathes exhilarating life into its tired premise, thanks to some dazzling action choreography and stylish visuals.
