Inception

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Year: 2010

Genre: Science-Fiction

Story

Cobb steals information from his targets by entering their dreams. He is wanted for his alleged role in his wife's murder and his only chance at redemption is to perform a nearly impossible task.

Review

“Inception” is a triumph, a perfectly oiled dream machine that can blow minds on first viewing and satisfy cinephiles on repeat viewings.Nolan at his most intelligent best. Inception is truly one of a kind.It's a heist film, a tragic romance, science-fiction, and an action film. But the best thing is it uses all of these ingredients flawlessly.
