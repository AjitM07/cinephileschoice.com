IT

Director: Andy Muschietti

Release Year: 2017

Genre:Horror

Story

Seven helpless and bullied children are forced to face their worst nightmares when Pennywise, a shape-shifting clown, reappears. The clown, an ancient evil torments children before feeding on them.

Review

It is a standard monster movie, and the monster is Pennywise who kills a lot of people. Then, the monster is scary only if it's not on screen every two minutes. It was a great flick about friendship and adventure and the child actors were excellent.It: Chapter One" (2017) is a well-directed and atmospheric horror film that effectively captures the essence of Stephen King's novel, focusing on a group of kids terrorized by the supernatural clown Pennywise in 1980s Derry, Maine.
