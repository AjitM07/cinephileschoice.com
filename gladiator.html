Gladiator

Director: Ridley Scott

Release Year: 2000

Genre: Action

Story

Commodus takes over power and demotes Maximus, one of the preferred generals of his father, Emperor Marcus Aurelius. As a result, Maximus is relegated to fighting till death as a gladiator.

Review

This film has spectacular fight scenes in dazzling sets and a familiar story about honor and betrayal, courage and cowardice. A moving and very entertaining action epic with Russel Crowe and Joaquin Phoenix at the top of their game. The film is epic, moving, packed with great action, and excellently acted. The score is haunting and the visuals are mesmerizing. The film never fails to be involving and entertaining.
