Jurassic Park

Director: Steven Spielberg

Release Year: 1993

Genre: Adventure

Story

An industrialist invites some experts to visit his theme park of cloned dinosaurs. After a power failure, the creatures run loose, putting everyone's lives, including his grandchildren's, in danger.

Review

The story is great, how at first we see how the magic was created, and then later we see the magic turn into a disaster, in which everyone's life is at stake. Lots of action and great special effects that have stood the test of time. The acting is good all around, and the plot is fantasy yet believable. The classic movie's unforgettable performances and CGI effects remain glorious 30 years after its original release. “Jurassic Park” turned out to be an incredible visual achievement and a groundbreaking step forward for movie technology.
