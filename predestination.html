Predestination

Director: Michael Spierig, Peter Spierig

Release Year: 2014

Genre: Science-Fiction

Story

As his last assignment, a temporal agent is tasked to travel back in time and prevent a bomb attack in New York in 1975. The hunt, however, turns out to be beyond the bounds of possibility.

Review

This twisting, turning, time-travelling thriller about the ability to change the course of destiny will leave you pondering long after the credits have rolled.Great cinematography and excellent acting contribute to this refreshing Sci-Fi/Time Travel film. Rising actress Sarah Snook with a stellar performance is backed by Ethan Hawk. The Spierig brothers show off a classy, if needlessly complex sci-fi that showcases a brilliant performance from newcomer Sarah Snook.
