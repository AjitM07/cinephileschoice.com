The Dark Knight

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Year: 2008

Genre: Thriller

Story

Batman has a new foe, the Joker, who is an accomplished criminal hell-bent on decimating Gotham City. Together with Gordon and Harvey Dent, Batman struggles to thwart the Joker before it is too late.

Review

What to Know...Dark, complex, and unforgettable, The Dark Knight succeeds not just as an entertaining comic book film, but as a richly thrilling crime saga. The pinnacle of Nolan's career so far, The Dark Knight is one of the most impressionable films of the 21st century (and one of the best). The Dark Knight is strange, dark, grandiose and mad; it is overlong and overhyped but hugely entertaining. In a simple, physical sense it really is huge.
