Logan

Director: James Mangold

Release Year: 2017

Genre: Superhero

Story

Logan comes out of retirement to escort a young mutant named Laura to a safe place. He meets with other mutants, who run from an evil corporation that has been experimenting with them, along the way.

Review

A comic book film done right. Gritty, honest and brutal. Just like a movie about Logan should be. This is by far the best X-Men film as far as I'm concerned. I love this movie for the grounded, gritty feel and the mature, depressing tone carries the movie. The film is stunningly bleak and staggeringly violent. Major characters go down in showers of blood and gore.“Logan” has stakes that feel real, and fight choreography that's fluid and gorgeous instead of just computer-generated effects.
