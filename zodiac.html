Zodiac

Director: David Fincher

Release Year: 2007

Genre: Thriller

Story

Robert Graysmith, a cartoonist by profession, finds himself obsessively thinking about the zodiac killer. He uses his puzzle-solving abilities to get closer to revealing the identity of the killer.

Review

Based on the wonderful novel, this film brilliantly tells the story of the unknown Zodiac killer, who operated in the late 60's and early 70's. Really good and scary thriller. A quiet, dialogue-driven thriller that delivers with scene after scene of gut-wrenching anxiety. David Fincher also spends more time illustrating nuances of his film. It's an incredible look into real history with respect to the victims and investigators while also making a very competent and mature detective film.
