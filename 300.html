300

Director: Zack Snyder

Release Year: 2006

Genre: Adventure

Story

In the ancient battle of Thermopylae, King Leonidas and 300 Spartans fight against Xerxes and his massive Persian army. They face insurmountable odds when they are betrayed by a Spartan reject.

Review

Stunning, awesome, amazing, incredible - the superlatives continue to flow as I think about the impact of this film. The acting was good, and the script solid enough, all in all earning a rating of an 8, but as promised, tanned and bearded men with valor earns it a 10. "300" (2006) is a visually striking and action-packed film, based on the graphic novel by Frank Miller, that is worth watching for its stylized violence, epic battle scenes, and strong performances.
