Good Will Hunting

Director: Gus Van Sant

Release Year: 1997

Genre: Drama

Story

Will Hunting, a janitor at MIT, is a mathematical genius. But circumstances force him to join therapeutic sessions, where he confronts his past, re-evaluates his present and thinks of his future.

Review

The plot is really intriguing and very interesting. Extremely well written and acting is very well performed and feels very authentic. The writing is witty, brilliant and hilarious at times, both Matt and Robin Williams preform wonderfully from both a comedic and theatrical prospective. Both gritty and moving, Good Will Hunting is a powerful tale of realizing your potential.
