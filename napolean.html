Napolean

Director: Ridley Scott

Release Year: 2023

Genre: Biopic

Story

A look at the military commander's origins and his swift, ruthless climb to emperor, viewed through the prism of his addictive and often volatile relationship with his wife and one true love, Josephine.

Review

Napoleon has plenty of terrific acting -- and action. The battle scenes are bloody and tense, and the costumes and sets looks incredible. A sturdy but uneven historical epic that traces the rise and fall of Bonaparte (Joaquin Phoenix), starting with the French Revolution. Joaquin Phoenix is oddly mesmerizing as the French emperor in Ridley Scott's historical epic charting his rise and ruin. Every frame of Ridley Scott's Napoleon is reminiscent of the great masters.
