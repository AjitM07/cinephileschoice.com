Spiderman

Director: Sam Raimi

Release Year: 2002

Genre: Superhero

Story

Peter Parker, a shy and awkward high school student, is often bullied by people, including his best friend. His life changes when he is bitten by a genetically altered spider and gains superpowers.

Review

On its own, it's a very good superhero flick, alongside the sequel, it's an absolute masterpiece. Not only does Spider-Man provide a good dose of web-swinging fun, it also has a heart, thanks to the combined charms of director Sam Raimi and star Tobey Maguire. Definitely the movie was good on character development and complexity of friendship between Norman Osborn and Peter Parker but deadly enmity of their alter egos.
