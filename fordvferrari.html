Ford v Ferrari

Director: James Mangold

Release Year: 2019

Genre: Biopic

Story

Carroll Shelby, an automotive designer, and Ken Miles, a race car driver, join hands to build a trailblazing vehicle and compete against the Ferrari race cars.

Review

An exhilarating and emotional ride. A lot of notes were hit by Ford v Ferrari. It is an excellent sports drama that proves you don't have to be an expert on the rules to lose yourself in the game. The dialogue is replete with zingers and the racing sequences are a blast. Mangold sticks to the verities and conveys high speeds and potentially deadly impacts.
