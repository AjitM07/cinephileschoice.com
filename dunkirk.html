Dunkirk

Director: Christopher Nolan

Release Year: 2017

Genre: Thriller

Story

During World War II, soldiers from the British Empire, Belgium and France try to evacuate from the town of Dunkirk during a arduous battle with German forces.

Review

Dunkirk serves up emotionally satisfying spectacle, delivered by a writer-director in full command of his craft and brought to life by a gifted ensemble cast. Intense, challenging story shows the horrors of war. Those with fears of claustrophobia and drowning should steel themselves. A surprisingly great war film, this movie tells two different stories. One is about a group of British soldiers trapped behind enemy lines.
